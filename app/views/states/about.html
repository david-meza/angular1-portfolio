About Me!

A

Background

I have always been fascinated by technology. Since elementary school, I've been looking for ways to build cool things in a logical way. I remember my first interaction was drawing a house using Turbo Pascal with Turtle graphics when I was 6.

As time progressed I moved into more complex things. I attended college and graduated with a major in MIS, and during that time I supplemented my studies with online courses on programming.

Not being satisfied with the results of my education, I decided to attend Viking Code School shortly after graduation. VCS is a 15-week intensive program that teaches modern programming languages and frameworks to turn you into a professional full-stack web engineer.

Since then I've worked in multiple exciting projects at the City of Raleigh and continued building helpful tools and applications.
